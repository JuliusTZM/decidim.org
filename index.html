What is Decidim?

Decidim is more than a digital platform, it is a Free/Open project and infrastructure for participatory democracy. It involves code, documentation, design, training courses, legal frameworks, innovative practices, collaborative interfaces, development, user and facilitation communities, and a common vision. It helps society self-organize democratically at every scale: it enables citizens, organizations and public institutions (in districts, cities, regions, and beyond) to co-produce strategic planning, run participatory budgeting, construct multitudinous deliberation processes and enact distributed decision-making. Decidim boosts collective intelligence and action for the common good.

Participatory Processes made easy

Do you want to make a strategic plan? Or maybe to discuss new norms? Or perhaps have a debate around a new square or public building so that the common good is advanced?

Participatory Budgeting for Real

Online and Offline Hybridization

We are not only a digital platform, we're an application for people from all ages and origins. Decidim is designed to help to solve the digital divide and to create new forms of multi-layered, augmented participation.
